PROJECTS

Spectrum

A research project using artificial intelligence and immersive technologies as mediums in creating inclusive classrooms

The Disparities

A data-driven narrative that explores the intersectionality between climate change and women in different regions of Nepal.

Typography & Social Change

A booklet designed in InDesign exploring young typographers who use typography as a medium for social change.

Data Dystopia

A website educating people about online privacy and data surveillance in an interactive, storytelling way.

Reimagining MOHAI

This project explores the design process of developing an app platform for the Museum of History & Industry's Native Ground exhibit.

CharlieCardless

A mobile app that creates a more convenient public transportation experiences by providing users with digital access to their CharlieCard.

Sweet Dreams

Designing a multifunctional alarm clock that helps users improve their sleep and wellness through a fun, interactive experience.

U̶n̶t̶o̶u̶c̶h̶a̶b̶l̶e̶

A project created on ArcGIS Storymaps about casteism - exploring the rigid, Hindu caste system in Nepal and India.

Adding to ACE

Updating the 1995 Adverse Childhood Experiences (ACEs) questionnaire by researching new adverse factors and creating a data-driven source.

Tactically Framed

A platform, via an escape room event, that exposes how grave issues such as climate change have become "tactically framed".

College Unfiltered

College should not only be for the privileged. Through this project, we create useful content that can be used by all students to navigate through the college application maze.

Stop "Asian"

A digital campaign that strives to help people, who have been treated as a monolith by being labeled as “Asian”, discover their true identity outside of the way the West has defined them.
