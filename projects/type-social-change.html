Exploring Typography as a Medium for Social Change: A Booklet

I designed this booklet in Adobe InDesign, which delves into how young typographers are using type as a medium for social change. It highlights the work of Tré Seals and his type foundry Vocal Type Co., which creates typefaces inspired by marginalized voices and overlooked historical movements. It also explores designer Beatriz Lozano's boundary-pushing typefaces that incorporate AR and other technologies. Through compelling layouts and typography analysis, the booklet showcases these designers' commitments to diversity, representation, and social justice advocacy in the typography world. It serves as an inspirational look at how creative letterforms can give voice to important causes.
